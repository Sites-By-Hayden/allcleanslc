Our Cleaning Services

Air Duct Cleaning

Improve the air quality of your home, especially for those in your family who have respiratory health issues including emphysema, asthma, and allergies.

- Includes cleaning of all vents, air handlers, AC coils, and ducts; includes the handling of disconnected or broken ducts as well as bird and nest removal and the sealing of entry points for future prevention

- Average time is 6 hours from start to finish (for a one-furnace home)

- Costs range from $300 to $1000 (for a one-furnace home)

Carpet Cleaning

Create a healthy, odor-free environment while bringing beauty and longevity to your carpets.

- Includes furniture relocation, spot treating, deep cleaning (with hot water and suction)

- Average time is 30 minutes per room; time varies depending on size of rooms, condition of carpets, etc.

- Costs range from $50 to $70 per room with an average home costing $175 to $600; cost varies depending on size of rooms, condition of carpets, etc.

Tile & Grout Cleaning

Remove soil, oil and spots your bathrooms, kitchens, floors and more while eliminating bacteria and other harmful germs

- Includes safe, pH-balanced solutions, deep cleaning (with hot water and suction), sealers applied after cleaning

- Average time is 1 hour for rooms up to 300 square feet with a few hours of drying time; time varies on size of tile, dirt levels in the grout lines and room configuration and the number of pieces to be cleaned

- Costs range from $300 to $650 for a typical room with a price of $2 to $4 per square foot; cost varies depending on materials, cleaning method, damage levels, and need of restorative treatments

Area Rug Cleaning

Area Rug Cleaning Keep your high traffic runner free of dust and bacteria creating a welcoming aesthetic and prolonging the life of your area rug.

- Includes pick up and delivery, customized method to your unique rug, spot treating, deep cleaning, and drying

- Average time is 2-3 weeks; Area rug is picked up at your home, brought to our facility for cleaning, and delivered back to you

- Costs range from $50 to $350 with a price of $1.50 to $8 per square foot; cost varies depending on method of scrubbing, type of material, and size of rug
